Multilateral Diplomacy

Strategic engagement with international organizations including the UN, AU, and regional bodies to advance Kenya's global interests.

Peace & Security

Framework for maintaining international peace and security while safeguarding Kenya's sovereignty and territorial integrity.

Economic Diplomacy

Strategic approach to promoting trade, investment, and economic partnerships that support Kenya's development goals.

Cultural Diplomacy

Promoting Kenya's rich cultural heritage and values to foster understanding and build relationships globally.

Key Foreign Policy Areas

Regional Integration

Strengthening ties within East Africa and the broader African continent through active participation in regional organizations.

Global Governance

Contributing to international peace, security, and the reform of global governance structures.

Climate Diplomacy

Leading global efforts on climate change and environmental sustainability initiatives.

Digital Diplomacy

Leveraging technology and digital platforms to enhance diplomatic engagement and service delivery.
