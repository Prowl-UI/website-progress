Passport Services

Kenya E-Passport Information

Kenya E-Passport applications are processed online. The Embassy does not process passport applications directly but provides guidance and support.

Application Process:

  • Online application at immigration portal
  • Biometric enrollment required
  • Personal appearance mandatory
  • Valid for 10 years

Processing Centers:

  • London (UK)
  • Washington DC (USA)
  • Berlin (Germany)
  • Dubai (UAE)
  • Other select embassies
Note: For passport applications, Kenyan citizens in Thailand may need to travel to designated processing centers. Contact us for guidance on the nearest available center.
