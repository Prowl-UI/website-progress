Mission, Vision & Mandate

Our Mission

To promote and protect Kenya's interests abroad while providing excellent consular services to Kenyan citizens and facilitating stronger bilateral relations through diplomatic engagement, trade promotion, and cultural exchange.

Our Vision

To be a leading diplomatic mission that exemplifies excellence in service delivery, fosters sustainable partnerships, and positions Kenya as a preferred destination for investment, trade, and tourism in the global arena.

Our Mandate

To execute Kenya's foreign policy objectives, protect national sovereignty, advance economic diplomacy, provide consular services, and promote Kenya's image and interests in accordance with international law and diplomatic protocols.

Core Values

Excellence

Commitment to delivering high-quality services and maintaining professional standards in all our diplomatic activities.

Integrity

Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our interactions and representations.

Service

Dedicated to serving Kenyan citizens and fostering positive relationships with our host country.

Innovation

Embracing modern approaches and technology to enhance diplomatic effectiveness and service delivery.

Respect

Honoring diversity, cultural differences, and maintaining diplomatic courtesy in all engagements.

Accountability

Taking responsibility for our actions and being answerable to the people of Kenya and our stakeholders.

Strategic Objectives

Economic Diplomacy

Priority 1

Promote trade, investment, and economic partnerships to enhance Kenya's economic growth and development.

Consular Excellence

Priority 2

Provide efficient, timely, and professional consular services to Kenyan citizens abroad.

Peace & Security

Priority 3

Contribute to regional and global peace, security, and stability through diplomatic engagement.

Cultural Promotion

Priority 4

Showcase Kenya's rich cultural heritage, tourism potential, and national brand globally.

Commitment to Kenya

We are committed to advancing Kenya's interests with unwavering dedication, professionalism, and pride. Our work reflects the spirit of Harambee - pulling together for the common good of our nation and its people worldwide.
