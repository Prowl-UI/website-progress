Current Job Opportunities

Receptionist, Clerk and Clerk/Translator Positions

The Embassy of Kenya in Bangkok is seeking qualified candidates for three key positions: Receptionist, Clerk, and Clerk/Translator. These are contract-based positions for three years with possibility of renewal.

  • Contract Period: 3 years (renewable for another 3 years)
  • Application Deadline: September 19, 2025
  • Requirements: Bachelor's degree/Diploma, English & Thai proficiency, 2+ years experience preferred

The Embassy of Kenya in Bangkok welcomes expressions of interest from qualified individuals and organizations for various opportunities that may arise.

Required Information

Important Information

Your expression of interest should include the following information to be considered complete:

  • Full name and contact information
  • Area of interest (employment, partnership, etc.)
  • Relevant qualifications and experience
  • Brief statement of interest and objectives
