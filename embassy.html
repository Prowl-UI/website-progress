Welcome to Our Revamped Website

I have the honour to welcome you to the newly revamped website of the Embassy of the Republic of Kenya in Bangkok.

The website provides crucial information about the various activities of the Embassy as well as linkages to other Kenya government Ministries, Departments, and Agencies. Stakeholders and clients interested in learning more about trade and investment opportunities in Kenya will find comprehensive resources. Travelers and tourists will be able to get information about popular tourist destinations and tourist attractions in Kenya at the click of a button. The revamped website will also improve access to consular services for both Kenyan citizens and foreigners in need of such services.

The revamping of this website signifies our commitment to transparency, and accountability in the management of public affairs.

Through this platform, we aim to provide:
  • Timely and accurate information on our day-to-day activities
  • Updates on Kenya's foreign policy priorities, bilateral engagements, and regional initiatives
  • Resources to support trade, tourism, and investment between Kenya and the region
  • Cultural insights and news to promote mutual understanding and people-to-people exchanges

This is therefore a great opportunity for the Kenyan diaspora to stay connected and contribute to the mutual benefit of all our stakeholders.

As we continue to embrace digital diplomacy, I invite you to explore our new website and engage with our content. Your interaction is key to making this platform a valuable tool for all.

Our Establishment & Mission

The Embassy of the Republic of Kenya in Bangkok was officially established in 2006 as part of Kenya's strategic commitment to expanding its diplomatic presence in Southeast Asia. The Embassy serves as Kenya's principal mission to the Kingdom of Thailand and is concurrently accredited to Cambodia, Lao PDR, Myanmar, and Vietnam.

Since its establishment, the Embassy has played a pivotal role in strengthening Kenya's bilateral and multilateral relations in the region, fostering economic partnerships, promoting cultural exchange, and serving the interests of Kenyan citizens abroad.

Strategic Diplomatic Commitment

Our establishment represents Kenya's dedication to building stronger ties with Southeast Asia, recognizing the region's growing importance in global affairs and economic development.
