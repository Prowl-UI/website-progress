Passport Services

The Embassy provides comprehensive passport services for Kenyan citizens including new applications, renewals, and replacements.

Service Types

Required Documents:

  • Completed passport application form
  • Original birth certificate
  • Copy of national ID or birth certificate
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Payment receipt

Processing Time: 4-6 weeks
Fee: Contact embassy for current rates

Required Documents:

  • Completed passport application form
  • Original expired/expiring passport
  • Copy of national ID
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Payment receipt

Processing Time: 3-4 weeks
Fee: Contact embassy for current rates

Required Documents:

  • Completed passport application form
  • Police report (for lost/stolen passport)
  • Original birth certificate
  • Copy of national ID
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Copy of lost passport (if available)
  • Payment receipt

Processing Time: 4-6 weeks
Fee: Contact embassy for current rates
