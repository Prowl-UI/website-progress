Passport Services

The Embassy provides comprehensive passport services for Kenyan citizens including new applications, renewals, and replacements through the e-Passport system.

Application For E-Passport

Apply for the e-Passport at https://accounts.ecitizen.go.ke/en and pay for it online via mobile money, credit cards, debit cards or online banking. One will be required to fill in the form online.

General Information

Important Requirements

  • One must create an account on the e-citizen portal to access the passport application service
  • ALL Passport applications MUST be duly completed online via ecitizen.go.ke portal. Manual applications will NOT be accepted.
  • Please ensure you have read and understood the guidelines and Requirements before submitting your application.
  • ALL passport applications must be recommended by a Kenyan Citizen in possession of a Valid Passport/National Identity Card and who is not a relative on (section 7) of the application form.
  • All applicants are advised to apply for a passport replacement at least six months before its expiry date.

Dual Citizenship Requirements

  • Dual Citizens are required to declare their other nationality prior to renewing Kenya passports.
  • This includes persons born before 27th August 1987 and for those who acquired other citizenship status before 27th August 2010 need to show proof of having regained Kenya citizenship.
  • Those required to apply to regain Kenya citizenship are expected to await completion of the regaining process before they can apply for their e-passports.

Photograph Requirements

Kindly ensure that photos attached on the application form meet the following criteria:

  • Be clear photos – they should not be blurred nor have a dark hue, all facial features should be clearly outlined
  • Have a full face and both ears visible
  • Be taken against a WHITE background
  • Be without shadows in the background
  • No smiling or laughing
  • Be taken without spectacles
  • Size of the photos should be 5cm by 5cm (five by five centimetres)
  • Weaves and hair should not cover the forehead or the rest of the face
  • Decent photos – ladies should not be wearing strapless tops and earrings
  • Ensure the photos are printed on good quality paper in a studio
