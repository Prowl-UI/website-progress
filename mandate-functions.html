Mandate & Functions

Our Core Mandate

The Embassy of the Republic of Kenya in Bangkok operates under a comprehensive mandate to serve Kenya's national interests across multiple domains.

Primary Mandate

To execute Kenya's foreign policy objectives, protect national sovereignty, advance economic diplomacy, provide consular services, and promote Kenya's image and interests in accordance with international law and diplomatic protocols.

Core Functions

Our embassy performs essential diplomatic, consular, and economic functions to advance Kenya's interests in the region.

Diplomatic Representation

  • Serve as Kenya's principal diplomatic mission to Thailand
  • Represent Kenya in Cambodia, Lao PDR, Myanmar, and Vietnam
  • Maintain bilateral relations with host governments
  • Participate in multilateral diplomatic initiatives
  • Coordinate with international organizations

Consular Services

  • Issue emergency travel documents
  • Provide passport and visa services
  • Authenticate and legalize documents
  • Assist Kenyan citizens in distress
  • Coordinate repatriation services

Economic Diplomacy

  • Promote Kenya as a trade and investment destination
  • Facilitate business partnerships and joint ventures
  • Support Kenyan exporters and importers
  • Organize trade missions and exhibitions
  • Provide market intelligence and analysis

Cultural & Public Diplomacy

  • Promote Kenyan culture and heritage
  • Organize cultural events and exhibitions
  • Facilitate educational exchanges
  • Promote tourism to Kenya
  • Enhance Kenya's international image

Specific Responsibilities

Detailed breakdown of our key operational responsibilities

Protection of National Sovereignty

  • • Safeguard Kenya's territorial integrity and national interests
  • • Monitor regional security developments
  • • Coordinate with security agencies
  • • Report on matters affecting national security
  • • Participate in regional security initiatives

International Law Compliance

  • • Adhere to Vienna Convention on Diplomatic Relations
  • • Respect host country laws and regulations
  • • Maintain diplomatic protocols and procedures
  • • Ensure compliance with international treaties
  • • Uphold principles of international law

Diaspora Engagement

  • • Maintain diaspora registry and database
  • • Organize community events and meetings
  • • Facilitate diaspora investment in Kenya
  • • Provide welfare and emergency assistance
  • • Promote diaspora participation in national development

Administrative Functions

  • • Manage embassy operations and resources
  • • Coordinate with Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • • Prepare diplomatic reports and communications
  • • Maintain official records and documentation
  • • Ensure efficient service delivery
