National Anthem

The National Anthem of Kenya is "Ee Mungu Nguvu Yetu" (Oh God of All Creation). It was adopted in 1963 upon Kenya's independence.

English Version

Verse 1:
Oh God of all creation
Bless this our land and nation
Justice be our shield and defender
May we dwell in unity
Peace and liberty
Plenty be found within our borders

Verse 2:
Let one and all arise
With hearts both strong and true
Service be our earnest endeavour
And our homeland of Kenya
Heritage of splendour
Firm may we stand to defend

Verse 3:
Let all with one accord
In common bond united
Build this our nation together
And the glory of Kenya
The fruit of our labour
Fill every heart with thanksgiving

About the Anthem

The music was composed by a joint commission with members from Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Zambia. The lyrics reflect the hopes and aspirations of the Kenyan people for unity, peace, and prosperity.

The anthem emphasizes key values including:

  • Unity and brotherhood among all Kenyans
  • Justice and peace
  • Service to the nation
  • Gratitude and thanksgiving
  • Pride in Kenya's heritage
